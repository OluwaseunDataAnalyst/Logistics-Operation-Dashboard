# Logistics Operations & Profitability Dashboard

## Power BI Capstone Project
**By Oluwaseun Tijani**

## Project Overview

This Power BI project analyzes logistics operations, driver performance, fleet utilization, maintenance activities, customer profitability, and route performance.

The objective was to transform raw operational data into a comprehensive business intelligence solution that supports data-driven decision-making.

The final report consists of 5 interactive dashboard pages, providing insights into:

- Operational Performance
- Driver Performance & Efficiency
- Fleet & Maintenance Analysis
- Route & Customer Profitability
- Executive Summary & Recommendations

## Business Problem

Logistics organizations generate large volumes of operational data daily. Without centralized reporting, it becomes difficult to answer critical business questions such as:

- Are deliveries being completed on time?
- Which drivers perform best?
- Which trucks generate the highest revenue?
- How much does maintenance cost the business?
- Which customers contribute the most revenue?
- Which routes are most profitable?

This dashboard was developed to answer these questions and provide actionable business insights.

📂 Dashboard Pages
📈 Page 1 — Operational Performance Overview
Objective

Provide a high-level overview of logistics operations.

KPIs

✅ Total Loads

✅ Total Trips

✅ Total Revenue

✅ Total Miles

✅ On-Time Delivery %

Key Findings
Generated ₦263M in total revenue.
Completed approximately 80K trips and 85K loads.
Achieved an On-Time Delivery Rate of 56%.
Revenue is concentrated among a few high-performing origin cities.

Objective

Evaluate driver productivity, efficiency, and safety performance.

KPIs

✅ Total Drivers

✅ Average MPG

Key Findings
High-performing drivers generated significantly higher revenue.

🚛 Page 3 — Fleet & Maintenance Analysis
Objective

Monitor fleet utilization and maintenance performance.

KPIs

✅ Total Trucks

✅ Fleet Utilization Rate

✅ Maintenance Cost

✅ Downtime Hours

✅ Maintenance Events

Key Findings
Fleet utilization averaged 83%.
Total maintenance spending exceeded ₦6M.
Parts costs accounted for the largest maintenance expense.
Some trucks experienced significantly higher downtime.

🛣️ Page 4 — Route & Customer Profitability
Objective

Identify the most profitable customers and routes.

KPIs

✅ Total Customers

✅ Total Routes

✅ Revenue per Mile

✅ Average Revenue per Customer

✅ Average Revenue per Route

Key Findings
Revenue is heavily concentrated among a few high-value customers.
Contract customers contribute the largest share of revenue.
Top-performing routes generate over ₦10M each.
Revenue per mile remains relatively consistent across profitable routes.

📋 Page 5 — Executive Summary & Recommendations
Objective

Summarize business performance and provide strategic recommendations.

Recommendations
Improve on-time delivery performance through better scheduling and dispatch operations.
Prioritize preventive maintenance to reduce fleet downtime.
Focus on retaining high-value customers.
Optimize lower-performing routes to improve revenue per mile.
Provide targeted coaching for drivers with higher safety incident counts.
